About

To me nothing is more satisfying than sharing our thoughts and creations with others. Especially if at the end the other person walks off with happy thoughts and a smile.

Born 20 years ago in a village called Zwijndrecht on the outskirts of the Dutch Randstad.
Early in my youth, my parents and me moved to the neighbouring Hendrik-Ido-Ambacht, where I still live to this day.
During my high school period I obtained a CAE certificate and an International Baccalaureate, which allow me to operate on a higher level of English.
A Technasium certificate, which gave me an early taste at designing.
I finished high school with VWO NG&NT.

I am currently studying User Experience Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences.
Putting emphasis on understanding users.
Working as a freelancer alongside to improve myself in various fields.

When I’m not busy with work I can be found playing video-games or watching series and movies. But mostly I can be found reading up on what interest me. Be it work-related, game-related or a documentary on space.
